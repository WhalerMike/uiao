## Document Structure

This document originally contained a readable 15-page Introduction
(Sections 1–12) followed by detailed Appendices (A–J). Each section
includes "See Appendix X" references for detailed specifications.

The complete V4U document set comprises:

- **Sections 1–12** (this introduction): Executive summary and
  architecture overview.
- **Appendices A–J** (in CHARTER-003 / V4U Master Reference): Detailed
  technical specifications, crosswalk tables, and implementation
  guides covering the Paradigm Shift, Federal Mandate Crosswalk,
  Source of Authority chains, Federal Identity Fragmentation, and
  the NPE Assurance Model.

The Appendices A–J referenced above were merged into V4U_Master_Reference
(CHARTER-003) as the consolidated strategic-layer appendix file. The
appendix-level **technical detail** (Appendices A–CZ, ~104 appendices)
lives in the separately-tracked AtoBZ master appendix file
(CHARTER-001-APPENDICES, pending PR-A2b ingestion).

## Relationship to V4 audience-variant feeders

V4U was produced as the **unified merger** of three audience-variant
predecessors:

- **V4G** (Government audience) — provided FICAM/ICAM IAL/AAL/FAL framing.
- **V4P** (Private-sector audience) — provided the 17-Point Modernization
  Canon and private-sector comparison.
- **V4C** (Citizen audience) — provided the Four Core Conditions
  (Visibility / Verification / Validation / Control) framing.

The V4G / V4P / V4C source documents live in OneDrive
`Application_Aware_Networking_White_Paper_by_Mike/V4/Backup/` for
provenance reference. They are not separately ingested as canon —
their content is fully merged into V4U_Master_Reference (CHARTER-003).
