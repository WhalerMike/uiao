# UIAO — State-of-Tree Reconciliation and v0.6.0+ Candidate Themes

> **Status:** Draft, inbox-only. Not canon. Authored 2026-05-05 to
> reconcile a stale-branch session that produced ADR-057 (renumbered
> from a collided ADR-047) and an obsolete "OrgTree + Intune/Arc
> Readiness" v0.6.0 roadmap. This document reports what is *actually*
> on `origin/main` as of 2026-05-05 and lists candidate v0.6.0 themes
> the maintainer can pick from.

## 1. What this document is (and is not)

**Is:** A reconciliation report. Documents the gap between a session-internal
plan drafted from a 9-day-old branch base and the live state of `origin/main`.
Lists what landed on main while the session was running, what the original
roadmap proposed, and which proposals are now obsolete vs still useful.

**Is not:** A canon document. Not an ADR. Not a UIAO_NNN allocation. Not a
plan of record. The maintainer reviews this and either promotes a theme
into `src/uiao/canon/specs/` with a UIAO_NNN allocation + ratifying ADR, or
discards it.
