UIAO is under active development (v0.2.1). Much of this site describes target architecture — the canon is authoritative, but many components are not yet implemented, integrated, or operationally tested at scale. Pages describing capabilities that are canonically declared but not yet fully adopted carry an additional Aspirational callout at the top.
